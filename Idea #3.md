# idea #3 - Neon Dodge Blaster

## what is it?
a shooter game where u dodge bombs and also shoot enemies at the same time. kinda like a mix between bomb dodgers and space invaders but chaotic af

## the mechanics
- u have a ship/player that moves around the screen
- bombs/asteroids are falling down and u gotta dodge them
- but ALSO theres enemies shooting at u that u gotta shoot back
- the more u dodge and kill the higher ur score goes
- everything gets faster and more chaos happens as u progress
- power ups spawn randomly (shield, double damage, slow time, auto shoot, etc)
